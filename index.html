01 Food Delivery App

An online food delivery app that enables user to place and track their orders at ease. The saved database of the user improves the quality of service offered by the app. The costumers can check out recommended cuisines, restaurants, provide feedback and ratings which in turn can help improve the quality of service of the staff. A quick and easy payment methods are also available with ensured strict and secure privacy policies. The target group considered is 16+ (basically anyone with a smartphone)

1.Price discrepancies. Due to less transparency about the price mark ups of the restaurants and the app, it gives rise to misleading prices on food items.
2.Varying delivery time Excess delivery time can lead to cooling of food, which in turn leads to variation in taste.
3. Not having an option to customize recipes
